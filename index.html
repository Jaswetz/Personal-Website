About Me

I'm just a kid playing with his Legos...or at least that's how I feel.

I'm Jason and I've got a passion for creating. I enjoy the process as much as I do the final product (if there is such a thing). I believe in iteration and communication. I'm self-taught and never stop learning. My goal is to meet awesome people, create excellent things, and to continue growing.

What I do.

Design

My goal is to design aesthetically pleasing graphics, while creating readable and browsable content. With the advent of responsive design information architecture is becoming more important to the designer. I believe it is also important to create design patterns that focus on fluidity.

Front-End

As a front-end developer I love to solve problems and figure out how to do something better, faster. I focus on best practices and keeping things organized and accessible especially when working on a team. I keep in mind semantics and optimization. Recently my preference is to design in the browser, making it even more important to keep my files maintainable.

User Experience

I consider UX to be an extremely important (and fun) part of my workflow. Although I am not always able to quantify my decisions, I believe I have a robust understanding of the iterative process that produce's a better human experience with technology.
